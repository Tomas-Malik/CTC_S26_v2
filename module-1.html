AI Literacy 101 · Module 1

Knowledge Organization

From Mess to Message: turning everyday inputs into reports AI can actually help with.

90 minutes Level 1 Who it’s for: everyone

TL;DR

You’ll turn a pile of scattered, contradictory inputs — Slack messages, an email, a rough meeting note — into a clear report, twice: once anchored to a template, once built from scratch with AI as a thinking partner. Same mess, two techniques, one transferable pattern.

Prerequisites Computer literacy. No prior AI or prompting experience needed.
What you’ll need A laptop with internet access, and access to an AI assistant (ChatGPT or Claude).
Who it’s for Everyone — no technical background required.

After this module, you’ll be able to

  • 1 Use a template on purpose — show AI what counts as structure, tone, and signal vs. noise, instead of just pasting it in and hoping.
  • 2 Build structure with AI before generating anything, on tasks where you don’t have an example to work from.
  • 3 Catch what AI gets wrong with real, messy, contradictory inputs — the kind your actual inbox produces.
